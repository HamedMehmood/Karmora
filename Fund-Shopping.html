Step 1 - The Language of Fund-Shopping

Creating a Fund-Shopping Campaign is easy and should only take between 15-30 minutes depending on how much copy you already have in regards to your organization, its mission, and the fundraising campaign you are creating. But, before we get started let’s discuss some key Karmora terminology:

  • Fundraising Organization
    Is the organization approved for this Fundraising Webstore.
  • Ambassador
    Ambassadors are supporters that the (Name of FO) will directly invite to join your Fund-Shopping Community. Typical ambassadors are teammates,
    supporters, volunteers, club members, parishioners, fraternity brothers or sisters, etc...
  • Shoppers
    Shoppers are the supporters that your ambassadors will invite to join their Fund-Shopping communities. Your fundraising/shopping community has the potential to grow exponentially with Karmora.
  • Fund-Shopping Community
    With a Karmora Fundraising Webstore there are two ways to raise money for your worthy causes. It is a Fundraising Portal where your supporters can gift cash contributions; and it is a Cash Back Shopping Webstore where your supporters earn cash back on their purchases and Karmora gifts a percentage of every purchase to the (Name of FO); hence the name Fund-Shopping Community.

STEP 4 OF 5: SELECT A CAMPAIGN STYLE

Basic Fundraising Campaign (BFC)
Select a Karmora BFC if you do not intend to set fund-raising goals for your ambassadors and are content with inviting your existing list of supporters to join your shopping community and not much more. BFCs are for organizations with large communities like alumni asso-ciations, charities, and religious organizations. They are also ideal for teachers or smaller fundraisers with mod-est expectations.

Enter your total fundraising goal for this campaign. Please make sure that your goal is realistic for your support base. Supporters can be turned off by unrealistic fundraising goals and it can prove to be detrimental to your campaign.
Viral Fundraising Campaign (VFC)
Select a Karmora VFC if you are comfortable requesting your ambassadors raise a particular dollar amount and/or solicit a minimum number of FREE shoppers to join your shopping/fundraising community. VFCs are perfect for club sport teams; High School Sport, Club and Academic programs; small to midsize nonprofits; and basically any program with motivated ambassadors. With a VFC you can experience exponential growth in the number of shoppers in your community and the amount of dollars you can raise.
