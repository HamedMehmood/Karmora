Basic Fundraising Campaign (BFC)
Select a Karmora BFC if you do not intend to set fund-raising goals for your ambassadors and are content with inviting your existing list of supporters to join your shopping community and not much more. BFCs are for organizations with large communities like alumni asso-ciations, charities, and religious organizations. They are also ideal for teachers or smaller fundraisers with mod-est expectations.

Viral Fundraising Campaign (VFC)
Select a Karmora VFC if you are comfortable requesting your ambassadors raise a particular dollar amount and/or solicit a minimum number of FREE shoppers to join your shopping/fundraising community. VFCs are perfect for club sport teams; High School Sport, Club and Academic programs; small to midsize non-profits; and basically any program with motivated ambassadors. With a VFC you can experience exponential growth in the number of shoppers in your community and the amount of dollars you can raise.
