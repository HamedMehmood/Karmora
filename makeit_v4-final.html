How Much You Can Make As a Webstore Owner?

Attracting online shoppers that like to earn cash back and win CASH prizes for FREE shouldn't be difficult. It should be FUN! Keep it fun and watch how your community and income can grow! We've built a handy dandy calculator to help you estimate your potential income as a Webstore Owner.

 


STEP 1: Read through the Top prospect list descriptions and enter the number of shoppers you may have per category.
STEP 2: The average online shopper spends approximately $1,700 per year shopping online, but it can differ from family to family, business to business and charity to charity. How much will your shoppers spend every year on birthday & holiday gifts, back to school shopping, travel and vacation, office supplies and everyday household needs? You can change the average annual spend if you'd like to.
STEP 3: We like to use 6% as the average cash back rebate, however the percentage is different for every store. You can change the average cash back percentage if you'd like to.
STEP 4: Your estimated income will be automatically calculated. Karmora isn't a get rich quick scheme. As you add more shoppers your income will grow. Set a goal for the number of shoppers you want to have in your community and focus on having fun.

Friends, Family and Acquaintances – Add up the number of family members, friends, and regular acquaintances like waiters and waitresses, hair stylists, baristas, clerks, etc… that you think would be interested in earning cash back and winning FREE cash and enter that number. 

Stay at Home & Single Parents -  Add up the number of stay at home or single parents that you know who could use an extra $100, $200, $300… per month, who like to shop online and have fun.  Who knows, they may want to be a Webstore Owner just like you.  Enter that number.

College Students – College students understand social media and are always looking for a way to make some extra cash.  College students make great Webstore Owners.  Enter the number of college students you know that may be interested in earning cash back or learning more about becoming a Webstore Owner.

Alumni Associations – Karmora offers FREE Fundraising Webstores to Alumni Associations that could represent tens, hundreds, even thousands of shoppers earning you commissions.  For example… Your alumni Association has 10,000 members.  However, not all of them will be online shoppers or interested in supporting the alumni association.  Even if you estimated 10% of the alumni joining via the FREE Fundraising Webstore that would represent 1,000 shoppers in your community earning you commissions. 

Fraternities & Sororities – Karmora offers FREE Fundraising Webstores to Fraternities and Sororities that could represent a large number of shoppers joining your community.  For example… Your niece is a member of a sorority with 50 college students.  Each of the 50 gets their 5 friends and family to join for FREE and support their Sorority.  That is 250 new members in your shopping community potentially earning you commissions.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.    

Youth Sports Teams – Karmora offers FREE Fundraising Webstores to Youth Sports Teams and Clubs like baseball, football, basketball, cheer, volleyball, etc… Every parent you know probably has a child that is raising money for some program.  You are paid on every player/participant and every person they solicit to join and shop through their Fundraising Website.  Take the total # of players/participants on the team/club and multiply by 10 and enter that number.  For example… A 10 year old club football team has 20 players.  The coach asks each of the parent(s) to get 10 of their family members and friends to join for FREE and shop to support the team.  You now have 200 shoppers in your community potentially earning you commissions.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.   

High School Sports and Academic Clubs – High School sports teams and clubs love Karmora FREE Fundraising Webstores.  One team or club can represent tens, hundreds, even thousands of shoppers earning you commissions.  For example… a high school baseball team has 60 players between freshman, JV and Varisty.  As their fundraiser the coach asks each player/parent to sign up 10 friends and family members as free shoppers.  That's 60 players multiplied by 10 friends and family = 600 shoppers in your community.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.

Teachers – Karmora offers FREE Fundraising Webstores to teachers.  Teachers are always needing help with classroom supplies.  Not only will the teacher earn cash back, but she can raise money all year long for the next school year.  For example… a teacher has 30 students.  The teacher asks the parents to join her shopping community and purchase the classroom supplies and back to school items through their Free Webstore.  The parents will earn cash back and the teacher will be able to raise money for classroom supplies.  That's potentially 30 new shoppers in your community earning you commissions.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.

Religious Organizations – Karmora gives FREE Fundraising Webstores to Religious Organizations.  For example… Your church has 500 members.  Your pastor asks each parishioner to join the churches shopping community.  The parishioners will earn cash back on their purchases and the church will raise funds on every purchase.  That's potentially 500 members in your community earning you commissions.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.

Charities & Non-Profits – Karmora offers FREE Fundraising Webstores to Charities and Non-Profits.  Their supporters can join their shopping community and earn cash back on their purchases while helping the charity raise funds for worthy causes.  For example… A local charity supporting breast cancer awareness has 400 supporters.  That's potentially 400 shoppers earning you commissions.  However, not all of them may shop online, and you may want to reduce that number by a conservative percentage.

Small to Midsize Businesses – Every business, big or small, around you is buying office supplies and making travel arrangements.  With great stores like OfficeMax, Staples, Office Depot, Walmart, Sam's Club, Priceline, Travelocity, Budget Rental Car and many others, local business can be a great source of regular income.  Many businesses will spend much more than $1,700 per year.  And, what office manager wouldn't be interested in earning cash back on the purchases for themselves or their employer.  Enter the number of businesses you believe you can encourage to earn cash back.

Networkers & Multilevel Marketers – Karmora is not multilevel, but network marketers will understand the concept right away.  Becoming a Webstore Owner is inexpensive and can be an excellent supplement to their existing opportunity.  With Karmora they don't have to sell anything.  However, there are many other types of professional networkers that could use some extra cash such as realtors, car salesmen, and mortgage brokers.

Coupon Clippers & Bargain Shoppers – Bargain shoppers are excellent candidates.  They will love earning cash back and winning CASH prizes.  Bargain shoppers and Coupon Clippers make excellent Webstore Owners.
