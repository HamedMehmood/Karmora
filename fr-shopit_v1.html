Shopping with Karmora is a Win – Win

 

 

 

 

It's a Win for you!

You buy what you want, when you want it, from where you want it and earn Cash Back from your favorite stores on every qualified purchase. The prices are exactly the same because you are making your purchase from the store directly, you are just starting your journey at Karmora. It's really that simple.

 

It's a Win for United Way!

You are a member of United Way's Karmora Shopping Community. Karmora donates a percentage of every qualified purchase made by you, and every other member of their shopping community directly to United Way every month.


Win – Win

That's right! You can earn cash back on purchases from more than 1,700 stores that you already know, trust and love; and United Way gets paid every time you do!

HOW IT WORKS IN PLAIN ENGLISH
Karmora is an advertising portal. We are being paid to drive traffic to the websites of our advertisers. We are sharing that payment with our members. It’s that simple.

HOW IT WORKS IN PLAIN ENGLISH

When you select a Karmora store, such as Macy’s, and click Shop Now you are transferred to the advertiser’s website where you will shop and actually make your purchase.

 

But first you pass through a gateway we like to call a “handshake” with the advertiser. When you pass through this gateway a cookie is placed on your computer communicating your unique Karmora ID to the advertiser.

 

 

If you decide to make a purchase, the advertiser credits the cash back to your unique Karmora ID and issues the payment to Karmora, typically within 60 days. A percentage of that cash back is paid to all applicable members upon receipt of the payment from the advertiser.
