About Us

The Desert Mountain High School Wolves Wrestling Program has produced several State and Regional Champions including Mason Maksudian, Shane McGough, Danta Patrick, and many other.

Desert Mountain Wolves Wrestling is a tax exempt organization.  Please check with your individual tax advisor about the deducibility of your generous contribution. 

About Our Fundraising Campaign...

As athletic budgets continue to shrink fundraising has become a necessity to keep our program alive.   This year we are raising money to provide new wrestling uniforms, headgear and bags that are almost 12 years old.  Our wrestlers put in countless ours of some of the most demanding training of any high school sport and it would be great if they could feel good and take pride in their wrestling gear.  Any help you can lend is greatly appreciated!
