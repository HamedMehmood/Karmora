JOIN THE KARMORA FUNDRAISING REVOLUTION

Karmora FREE Fundraising Webstores are revolutionizing traditional fundraising. Supporters get to purchase what they want, from where they want, whenever they want and get cash back on their purchases, while at the same time supporting their favorite teachers, clubs, charities and religious organizations.

How it Works:


  1. Prospective fundraiser fills out the short request form below for review. The fundraiser must have either a website, Facebook page, and/or an educational email address (.edu) to qualify. The approval process typically takes between 24-48 hours.
  2. Fundraiser receives an approval email with instructions on how to set up their profile to customize their Fundraising Webstore and describe their fundraising goals. It's really simple.
  3. Fundraiser invites its support base to join their shopping community for FREE and earn cash back on their purchases using our custom email and social media Share Platform. Each supporter that joins will receive their very own Webstore. It's really simple.
  4. Supporters have 4 ways to contribute:
    • They can support the fundraiser by making a donation via credit or debit card.

  5. Fundraiser can manage their campaign and collect funds in the reporting section of their Webstore anytime they choose.
