Enter # of Shoppers
STEP 1:
Read through the Top prospect list descriptions and enter the number of shoppers you may have per category.
Enter Annual Spend
STEP 2:
The average online shopper spends approximately $1,700 per year shopping online, but it can differ from family to family, business to business and charity to charity. How much will your shoppers spend every year on birthday & holiday gifts, back to school shopping, travel and vacation, office supplies and everyday household needs? You can change the average annual spend if you'd like to.
Enter Average Rebate
STEP 3:
We like to use 6% as the average cash back rebate, however the percentage is different for every store. You can change the average cash back percentage if you'd like to.
Annual Commission
STEP 4:
Your estimated income will be automatically calculated. Karmora isn't a get rich quick scheme. As you add more shoppers your income will grow. Set a goal for the number of shoppers you want to have in your community and focus on having fun.
